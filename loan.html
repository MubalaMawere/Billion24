Loan Terms and Conditions

Before submitting your application, please review our loan policies carefully:

  • All loans are collateral based and must be backed by a verifiable asset.
  • Students can borrow up to a maximum of K3,000 only.
  • Interest rates depend on the selected repayment term:
    • 1 Week – 13%
    • 2 Weeks – 20%
    • 3 Weeks – 30%
    • 4 Weeks – 35%
  • Loan approval is processed within 24–48 hours after successful collateral verification.
  • Repayments must be made on or before the agreed due date to avoid additional charges or penalties.
  • Failure to repay may result in the forfeiture of the collateral asset, as outlined in the agreement.
  • Upon disbursement of funds, the contractually agreed interest rate becomes fixed and will not be altered thereafter.
  • All personal information provided is treated as confidential and is used solely for loan processing and verification purposes.

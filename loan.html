Loan Terms and Conditions

Before submitting your application, please review our loan policies carefully:

  • All loans are collateral-based and must be backed by a verifiable asset.
  • Students can borrow up to a maximum of K3,000 only.
  • Interest rates depend on the selected repayment term:
    • 1 Week – 10%
    • 2 Weeks – 20%
    • 3 Weeks – 25%
    • 4 Weeks – 35%
  • Loan approval takes 24–48 hours after collateral verification.
  • Repayments must be made on or before the agreed due date to avoid penalties.
  • Failure to repay may result in the forfeiture of the collateral asset.
  • All personal information submitted is kept confidential and used only for loan processing.
